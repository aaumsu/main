Welcome to the Official Site of AAU - MSU

AAU Muslim Students Union is the union of the student jemeas found in the different campuses of Addis Ababa University.

Our Contacts

Activities of the Union

The union engages in different activities to help the students in the University and the whole Muslim community

Da'wah and Islamic Teaching

The union and the campus jemeas facilitate the da'wah and qira'at programs that are offered to the muslim students in the University. This qira'at programs include Daily Qur'an Recitation education for beginners and halaqas of different books thought by students and also invited mashaikhs. The union also engages in da'awah programs where, in addition to the weekly campus level programs, it prepares periodic da'wah programs in which students from all campuses participate.

Academic and Training Events

The Union offers tutorials for its students to improve their education. It is also planning on helping elementary and highschool students in their academics. It is also planning on preparing life skill trainings for students to improve their skills.

Community Services

The Union and the campus jemeas prepare events in which students can help their community.
